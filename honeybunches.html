Honey Bunches of Oats

Honey Bunches of Oats: Family Game Night edition is a rebrand of the original Honey Bunches of Oats cereal. The edition features the same classic cereal, with a interactive box. The project focuses on how to utlilze the cereal box as a board game intended for young families to play together.

Research

The reasearch for this prject consisted of diving into the Honey Bunches of Oats brand history and identityt. Establishing what the current brand does was esstential to mantaining cohesion wihtin the edition. The brand centers on family, fun, and inclusivity. The vibrant colors and consistency of the box designs reflect these ideals. Below are boxes that shaped the final edition, ranging from 1989 to today.

Design Elements

The final edition consists of a redesigned dieleine and character stickers for within the box. The color palette draws on the theme of nighttime, while keeping the roginal brand colors. The central logo on the front acts as a spinner, complete with tasks to win the game.
